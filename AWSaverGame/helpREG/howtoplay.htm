A race against time...

There are 6 levels in total - 3 on land and 3 in space. You must shoot all enemy fighter ships on each level before you run out of time. Fail to do so and it's back to the start of the level again - but if you get all the gnats, you'll move on to the next level...

Playing 'SpaceGnats - the SaverGame™':

Start AlteredSaver v3!

Double click on the new icon on your desktop,



or select Programs, AlteredWorlds, AlteredSaver v3!

Now chose the 'Game' page and press 'Play Now!'


In the bottom right hand corner of the game you'll see a green bar - this represents how much time you have left. It grows smaller as time progresses and when it is no longer visible an alarm will sound and the level will reset itself back to the beginning.

The overall goal is to kill the ships fast enough so you make it through all 6 levels to the time tunnel, leading you back to your own dimension...

*Added bonus* the screen saver remembers where you made it to and evolves with your game - the futher you progress, the more you'll see...
