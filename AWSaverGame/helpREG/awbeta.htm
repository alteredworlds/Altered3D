AlteredSaver v3 BETA!

AlteredWorlds are proud to announce the release of a beta version of our interactive 3D screen savers for Windows and Linux, for the first time including our 3D Studio MAX Export plugin, allowing users to create their own AlteredSaver scenes!

Help us create a new real time art community – interactive art for expanding minds worldwide!

http://www.AlteredWorlds.com/updates/AlteredSaver3.exe [5.6MB]

Features:

Still runs well on older hardware. Captures objects, animations, textures, lighting from 3D Studio MAX. With an NVIDIA GeForce or better, AlteredSaver now includes the following features, accessible in a natural manner from 3D Studio MAX:

  1. Cubic Environment Mapping
  2. Per-Pixel Dot3 Bump Mapping
  3. Enhanced OpenGL specular lighting (GL_EXT_separate_specular_color)
  4. Full use of Transform & Lighting.

All of our award winning 3D content was created using earlier versions of this Exporter plugin. It’s not perfect, but we find it reliable, fast and above all great fun to use. View your scene immediately after the export is complete, with just one mouse click! Ultra-rapid iteration time!

This BETA version of AlteredSaver, the viewer for our ‘.tnt’ content, is FREE and available NOW for both Windows and Linux.
